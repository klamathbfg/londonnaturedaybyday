Welcome to London Nature day by day

About London Nature day by day

Hey London, have you ever stopped to think about the incredible nature hidden right here in our bustling city. Well get ready because on January 1st 2025 I am launching something truly special in memory of the Alan Christrian Palfrey a founding member of the plant life charity.

Imagine this every single day for an entire year a new episode drops on X/Twitter, Facebook and YouTube revealing the secrets of London’s biodiversity. That’s right 365 episodes packed with fact, tips and stories about the environment around you.

From the rare plants tucked away in Hamsted Heath to the vibrant wildlife thriving along the Thames you will discover a new layer of our city that you never knew existed. Each Episode is designed to help you understand the importance of biodiversity on that specific day of the year.

You’ll learn how to spot seasonal changes, identify local species and appreciate the delicate balance of our urban ecosystem but this isn’t just about watching videos it’s about becoming part of a community. Share your experiences, join the conversation and let’s celebrate the natural beauty of London together. Whether you’re a nature enthusiast, a curious Londoner of someone looking to reconnect with the outdoors this series is for you.

So why am I doing this. To honor Alan Christan Palfreys legacy and to inspire you to see our city through a new lens. Biodiversity isn’t just a buzzword it’s the heartbeat of our environment and every day there is something new to learn and appreciate.

So mark your calendars for the 1st of January 2025, subscribe, follow and get ready to embark on a year long journey into the hidden wonders of nature. Let’s make 2025 a year of discovery, learning and connection with the world around us.

London are you ready to unlock the secrets of nature daily? I can’t wait to share this adventure with you, see you on January 1st. Don’t forget to like, share and subscribe to stay updated.
